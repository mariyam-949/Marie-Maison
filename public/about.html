Our Maison

Three generations
of quiet craft.

Marie Maison was founded in Paris in 1948 by Marie Lavigne — a goldsmith devoted to a single idea: that a piece of jewelry should outlast its owner. Today, three generations later, every piece is still hand-finished in our atelier on rue de la Paix, using the same patient methods.

Heritage

Family-owned since 1948. Every piece signed, every stone traceable.

Materials

18k gold, platinum, certified diamonds and ethically sourced gemstones.

Atelier

Hand-finished in Paris. Lifetime servicing on every piece.
